Industry Challenges
  • The design & thus BOM change in every order
  • Customer approval of drawings & QAP may be required.
  • Customer demands faster delivery
  • It is important to monitor profit in each order
  • Dependency on skilled people to execute orders
  • Good talent is hard to find
  • Global competition squeezing margins

We understand these challenges & process driven approach is the answer. We help you with setting up the right processes through OurSys.
