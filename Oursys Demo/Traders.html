ERP for Trading Companies

Industry Scenario

Majority of the businesses are generally Traders. They are of all sizes from single item traders to importers to technical item stockists. Some do not carry inventory and some on the other hand have multiple gowdowns at multiple locations.

Traders have a very fast business cycle and the margins are slim. Their income depends on how efficiently the money is rotated. Traditionally these were family businesses and ran on trust rather than systems. But in the age of digitisation some of the trading communities is now thinking about process driven approach and software beyond accounting. They now realise the value of a smart operations management software and have started adopting the change.

At Momenta we understand their challenges and opportunities and we have the right experience to deliver the right ERP for such companies and also help them in implementing it.

OurSys provides the features that make life easier at these units by deskilling the processes. OurSys integrates all departments and creates synergy. Have a look at our modules and the special features that we have developed over a long time working with various such companies.
