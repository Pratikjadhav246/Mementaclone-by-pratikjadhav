Industry Challenges
  • Two step production where first part is compounding and the second part is mechanical.
  • Wide variety of applications of rubber from tiny O-rings to gigantic metal bonded products
  • Maintaining secrecy of compund formulations
  • Die life monitoring is critical as Dies are costly
  • Production is often outsourced and die movement has to be managed
  • Compound Batch wise tracebility is important
