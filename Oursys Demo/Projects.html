ERP for Project based Companies

Industry Scenario

This product has been specially designed for companies who are majorly into projects. Engineering service providers, Contrators, Professionals, Consultants, manufacturing companies, digital marketing companies, event management companies etc. can all take the benefit.

We understand that top end Project Management software are sometimes overwhelmingly complex and fail to get implemented in SMEs. Also these software are independent project management software and do not have other modules which are required by the company. We have designed our product to cater to these SME project management companies.

OurSys project management is simple to use and has other ERP modules such as CRM, Accounts, HR, Inventory etc. as it is a full-fledge ERP at the heart. OurSys integrates all departments and creates synergy. Have a look at our modules and the special features that we have developed over a long time working with various companies.
